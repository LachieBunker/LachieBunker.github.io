Fight the Infection

About

Date: November 2017-January 2018

The idea for Fight the Infection came when I was recovering from being sick, and wanted to make a game that involved fending off waves of infection. I was pretty happy with the balance I was able to strike between players learning how to fight, and repeatedly encountering the same enemies in a level, with having a whole new set of enemies to fight each time they restarted.
