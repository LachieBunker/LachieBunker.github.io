Antidote

About

Date: May-July 2016

Antidote is a puzzle-platformer I made in my spare time during uni. I initially made it with 20 levels, but have since worked on it, and now it has 50 levels. I showcased Antidote at both my uni's symposium, and the end of semester showcase, and at both events it was well received, and I got lots of positive feedback.

Fair warning, some of the levels can be very stressful
